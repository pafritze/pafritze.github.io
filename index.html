ThemeGlider meets GenerativeAI

The role of metacognition in the era of AI

Some Thoughts...

  • traditional thematic analysis of complex data
  • seductive nature of ai generated interpretation
  • opportunities of ai in creating flexible tools
  • importance of bringing human creativity into the equation

The original development of the ThemeGlider iOS app was carefully designed to help people understand the views of subjects, clients, or customers, or explore meaning within text - with minimum fuss. It was founded on long experience and genuine passion for pragmatic qualitative research and software design. The release of ChatGPT turned my thinking on its head. How does this thematic analysis sit in this new world? The opportunities are profound.
